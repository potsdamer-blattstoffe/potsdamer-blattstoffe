Wozu 605?

These are stations inset.
They had a laird: “wherefore”.
Awhile the mildew of fear
- Each boot-protest, near and constant -
Lines other rubber forms reset.

How now they are upon
A vehicle inviting us
To love-in-idleness or pansy sonatas.
Beneath or down the wing: a worm’s cobstone.

I, all to do to turn it round, step out
And hold my hand up right.
I am in front of officer or homeless stray.
I am now in the wait of every day.
