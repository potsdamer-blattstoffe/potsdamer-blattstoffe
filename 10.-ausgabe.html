but the off the cuff hasn't
      been barred, the cuff
is off and punching
       beyond the moon,
       out to the stars,      
to lunch on them
too, collaring, button
holing
the universe.
the one size, the biggest yet
to not fit anyone
is appropriating
everything ever & constantly
approaching, encroaching
closer & closer
with its sacrificial odour
of sanctity, which need only
be a simile, the grimace
and simulacrum of a clown
or several, but with no humility,
     absolutely
no sense of ditch

from the top of a heap
     even if of bullshit,
     paying bullshit
and before a circular crowd
that perhaps pays attention
i guess, and maybe buys it, paying
in blood
not its own, its own
and maybe doing
something else as well —
ore even, something quite nice,
heating up the oven,
driving up the reverses
of disappointment backwards
the door of it is so
hot, the gates straight, so
      hard, so rigid —
cowboy & clown are staring
honesty down over their plate edge being
     the absolute tray edge
of their
horizon, whose pod can be
 luxury, depravity
and a screen, on which one can
toilet cheat comfortably
and gamble and
the narrows may be mined
but the mind is so narrow
that it can, it can
slip through all the damned
on its hazards,
its might

the smashing machine
that will cure no ills
but will, for a very very few, pay the bills

envoy. who owns the wind, the sea?
the sky       who owns the
dinosaurs and broken down ferns, bones
of the earliest humans
are twisting, are woven
in this global frenesy tristesse
and silence, barking, guffawing.
are resisting, are the flames
of ourself on the doorstep
of the fortresses the footrest
of shadows
