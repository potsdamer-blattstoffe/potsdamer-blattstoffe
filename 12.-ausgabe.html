Pantheon
To Virginia, where I have never stepped foot
 
Round both inside and out
Or is this a mistake?
At day, at night,—- here, I take it to be the way I see it always is,
 
From the inside. Vacant,
Some panels loosened and
Accumulating in the general sense of the place, in the abstract idea of the place,—-
 
Its seamless detentions
Of mind. I say, “Questions,
Are sounding just like promises because I am en scène, and there is nothing to be done. Oh well.”
 
To hear or to think, then,
Yourself means language can
Be spoken in American in every case. The dark red wine is still colour for lungs.
 
The unremembered dream,
Nothing. The adoption,
Altered. But how I give it to myself out of the aurora is true. The father, Tantalus,
 
Sipping the bath water
Reaching for the better
Variety of fruits,-- he found the children long ago, inside a bowel. They are Homerus, Ovidius, 

And everyone,—- Americans.
Also the French, Russians,
Who have never been to America,—- they speak American.
