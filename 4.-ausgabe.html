 With the Incense of the Angels 

My God, my God,
is the house on fire?
Is it time to raid
our closeted desire?

Like a caesarean
we hid between
the elements
of the salt plane:

the hydrogen and the chlorine
in clean production
since the angels set down and began
to burn their wings.
